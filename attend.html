Abstract Submission

Abstract submission deadline: February 24, 2025

Abstract submission guidelines

  1. Abstracts are limited to 250 words max.
  2. Late abstracts will not be accepted.
  3. Abstracts may be revised via the submission form up until the deadline.
  4. Submitters will receive an email notification regarding selection status.

Information for Presenters

Poster Printing Submission Deadline: March 14, 2025
Talk Upload Submission Deadline: March 18, 2025

Poster Guidelines

  1. We strongly encourage you to seek poster printing resources at your home institution.
  2. Posters for printing may only be submitted upon confirmation of abstract selection.
  3. Maximum physical size format is 4ft x 6ft.
  4. Maximum digital size format is 32gb.
  5. Presenters are strongly encouraged to attend the Science Communication workshop on March 3.
  6. No revisions will be accepted after March 14 for poster printing.

Speaker Guidelines

  1. Talks may only be submitted upon confirmation of abstract selection.
  2. Powerpoint or PDF only, slide size widescreen (16:9).
  3. Maximum time for talks is 12 minutes with 3 additional minutes for questions.
  4. Presenters are strongly encouraged to attend the Science Communication workshop on March 3.
  5. No revisions will be accepted after March 18.
  6. In-person presentation slides will be managed by the organizers, and will provide a clicker to advance slides.
