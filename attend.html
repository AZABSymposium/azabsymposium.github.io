Attend & Present

Abstract Submissions due by January 5, 2026

  • Submit an Abstract Here

The abstract submission form will also serve as your registration. A separate registration form for those not submitting abstracts will open on January 1, 2026.

Abstract Guidelines

  1. Abstracts are limited to 1500 characters (200-250 words).
  2. Late abstracts will not be accepted.
  3. Abstracts may be revised via the submission form up until the deadline listed above.
  4. You will receive an email notification by the end of January regarding selection status.

Presentation Guidelines

  1. Presentation constraints have not yet been finalized. In previous years, poster dimensions were 4 ft x 4 ft, and talks were allotted 12 minutes, followed by 3 minutes for questions.
